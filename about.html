ABOUT STEPS

Our company, STEPS, has been a well known name in outdoor shoe industry since the early 2020. Founded in United Kingdom, entered Indian market in 2022. Before that, STEPS was majorly exporting its leather shoes to Russia. After division of Russia in various states, the group decided to launch some of its products in India.

Adventure that defines Us

Over the years, the appetite for adventure sports has been on the rise. It is the spirit of adventure that defines Woodland. A spirit that is willing to stretch the levels of human endurance; a spirit that desires to reach the furthest frontiers; a spirit that craves to venture into the unknown.
